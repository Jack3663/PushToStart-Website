About The Mimic

The Mimic is a platformer puzzle game which boasts unique gameplay and innovative mechanics. With brain teasing levels, The Mimic will question what is to be a normal platformer. You will have to solve increasingly challenging levels where the rules could change at any time.

The Mimic will be available soon on Android, iOS and Steam
